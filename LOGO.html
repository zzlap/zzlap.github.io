PEOPLESQUARED

This logo is of my brand, on which I am working on. The idea of this logo is to be ever changing the identity is a stickman figure interacting with the box. I chose to make it ever changing to convey the idea that this brand is allways changing, it can relate to many different types of people. It can relate to those who are feeling sad, to those who are feeling adventurous, to those who are just lost, in a sense to everyone not specifically targeting the groups such as punks, anime fans or skaters. Allthough the stickman has the freedom to adapt to any idea if in the future I would desire to.
